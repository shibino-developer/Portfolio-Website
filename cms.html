College Management System

  • Home(current)

This comprehensive web application is tailored to streamline and enhance various administrative and academic processes within educational institutions.

Key Features:
Student Information Management:
Efficiently manage student records, including enrollment details, academic history, and personal information.
Course and Curriculum Management:
Seamlessly organize and update course offerings, class schedules, and curriculum content for improved academic planning.
Attendance Tracking:
Implement an automated attendance tracking system, providing real-time insights into student attendance and participation.
Grading and Evaluation:
Simplify the grading process with a user-friendly interface for entering, calculating, and managing student grades.
Faculty Information System:
Manage faculty details, teaching assignments, and performance evaluations for a transparent and organized faculty management system.
Online Assignment Submission:
Facilitate a digital platform for students to submit assignments and for faculty to provide feedback, streamlining the evaluation process.
